I have had a lot of joys and times when I thought I was going to give up during my gardening experience this summer!

    Lessons Learned
  • The seedling starting and transplanting is the easy part
  • There are repellants that actually work to keep deer and rabbits away!
  • The hardest part of farming is bug control
  • One has to be careful about some of the 'organic safe sprays' from the store,for bug control. They killed a lot of my plants!
  • You have to have a passion for gardening to do it. It's a lot of work and I have enjoyed every minute of it!
